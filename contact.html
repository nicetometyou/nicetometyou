Hi, nice to meet you. I am Michela Parma, the creator of this project. I was born in Rimini, on the east coast of Italy, an area called Romagna, where people love to drink Sangiovese and eat piadina. Since I lived in this wonderful land for 20 years, I chose to work on an ancient tradition in my area: rust printing. I have a historical background that allows me to do in-depth research in archives and libraries and discover fascinating things about our past.
I am currently enrolled in the second year of Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge, and this new university career has led me to discover 3D animation and web page creation, so I decided to apply my knowledge of the past and technology in this project.

If you want to contact me, please write at michela.parma4@studio.unibo.it, and if you want to have a look to other university projects, you can find them in my Github page.
